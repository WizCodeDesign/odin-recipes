Salad

Salad is a healthy dish made with a variety of fresh vegetables, fruits, nuts, and dressings.

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Bell peppers
  • Carrots
  • Avocado
  • Nuts (walnuts, almonds, or pecans)
  • Dressing (olive oil, balsamic vinegar, or your choice)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry the mixed greens thoroughly.
  2. Chop the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, bell peppers, and carrots into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Slice the avocado and set aside.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, chopped vegetables, and nuts.
  5. Drizzle with your choice of dressing and toss gently to combine.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Top with sliced avocado before serving.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your fresh salad!

Notes

Feel free to customize your salad with additional ingredients like cheese, croutons, or fruits.

Store any leftover salad in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days.

Refrigerate the dressing separately to keep the salad fresh.
