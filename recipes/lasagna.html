Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and a pinch of salt.
  6. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat mixture, mozzarella cheese, and marinara sauce. Repeat layers.
  8. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly.
  10. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
